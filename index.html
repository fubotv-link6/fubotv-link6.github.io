Activate FuboTV with fubo.tv/link6

Your step-by-step guide to get FuboTV running on any device ✅

What is fubo.tv/link6?

When you install the FuboTV app on your TV, streaming stick, or console, the app displays a unique activation code. Instead of typing your credentials on a TV remote, you can go to fubo.tv/link6 using your phone or computer and enter the code to connect your device instantly.

How to Activate FuboTV: Step by Step

  1. Install the FuboTV app: On your Firestick, Roku, Apple TV, or Smart TV — search and install “FuboTV”.
  2. Open the app and get your code: Launch FuboTV and note the unique activation code displayed.
  3. Visit fubo.tv/link6: On your phone, tablet, or PC, open a browser and go to fubo.tv/link6. Log in with your FuboTV credentials.
  4. Enter your activation code: Type in the code shown on your TV and hit “Submit” or “Continue”.
  5. Start streaming: Your TV or device will refresh and log in. Enjoy live TV, shows, movies, and sports!

Troubleshooting & Tips

If you face issues, try the following:

  • The code might expire — refresh the FuboTV app to get a new one.
  • Ensure your device has a strong, stable internet connection.
  • Update or reinstall the FuboTV app if it’s crashing or not working.
  • Logout and restart the process if nothing else works.

Supported Devices

FuboTV via fubo.tv/link6 works on:

  • Amazon Fire TV / Firestick
  • Roku devices
  • Apple TV
  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony, etc.)
  • Android TV boxes & more
